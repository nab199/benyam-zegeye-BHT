## 🚨 Common SEO Pitfalls to Avoid

### Technical Issues
- **Duplicate content**: Use canonical tags
- **Broken links**: Regular site audits
- **Slow loading**: Optimize images and code
- **Mobile issues**: Responsive design testing

### Content Mistakes
- **Keyword stuffing**: Natural language use
- **Thin content**: Comprehensive service descriptions
- **No local focus**: Geographic targeting essential
- **No updates**: Regular content additions

### Local SEO Errors
- **Inconsistent NAP**: Name, Address, Phone number
- **No reviews**: Encourage client testimonials
- **Wrong categories**: Proper business classification
- **No photos**: Add equipment and facility images
